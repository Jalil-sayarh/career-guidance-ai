# career-guidance-ai
We want to solve the uncertainty and lack of information students have from the end of middle school to high school, where they really start making decisions that affect their future. 

The small amount of information given to students usually comes from family and biased or unknowledgeable student counsellors. Those individuals don’t necessarily have the full picture of the state of the current/future job market, and how it’s matching with the student’s interests and aspirations. Leaving the student lost in his choices, without taking the right steps to know what he wants in the first place.

We aim to change that by providing accurate, unbiased, and accessible career and education guidance—helping students make informed decisions with confidence.
